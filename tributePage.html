A Timeline of Jimi's Life

  • Nov 27th 1942 - Jimi Hendrix is born.
  • May 31 1961 - Jimi is forced to enlist in the army after getting in trouble with the law.
  • Oct 15 1965 - Jimi signs a record contract with Ed Chalpin.
  • May 12 1967 - Jimi releases 'Are you Experienced' with The Jimi Hendrix Experience. In 2003, Rolling Stone magazine ranked it #15 on Rolling Stone's 500 Greatest Albums of All Time.
  • Dec 1 1967 - Jimi releases 'Axis: Bold as Love' with The Jimi Hendrix Experience. It reached #5 in the UK and later, #3 in the US.
  • Sep 16 1968 - Jimi Hendrix releases 'Electric Ladyland' The Third and final album from The Jimi Hendrix Experience. The album is seen as the peak of Hendrix's mastery of the electric guitar, and is frequently described as one of the finest albums ever recorded.
  • Sep 18 1970 - Jimi Hendrix Dies in London at the age of 27.
